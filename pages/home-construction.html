PAYMENT SCHEDULE

Financial Sequencing

Stage 1 — Mobilization Advance: 20%
Paid on agreement signing to mobilise the site team, secure initial materials, and set up temporary site works.
Stage 2 — Basement Completion: 20%
Payable after completion of basement slab and associated basement structural works, verified by the site engineer.
Stage 3 — Roof Slab: 20%
Raised after completion of the topmost floor roof slab and primary waterproofing to protect the structure.
Stage 4 — Brick Work: 10%
Covers brick work including brick/block laying for walls, bonding, and lintel placement verified by the site engineer.
Stage 5 — Plaster Works: 10%
Payable on completion of internal and external plaster works, surface preparation for finishes, and curing checks.
Stage 6 — Flooring & Fitouts: 10%
Covers floor finishes, tiling, electrical & plumbing points, and installation of modular fitouts and fixtures.
Stage 7 — Handover: 5%
Final payment at handover after snag clearance, painting, and issuance of the Defect Liability Bond and completion certificates.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the home construction cost per sq ft in Chennai 2026?+

Home construction costs in Chennai in 2026 typically range from ₹1,800 to ₹3,200 per sq. ft. depending on specifications. Morning Star Infra offers 4 packages: Basic (₹1,980), Classic (₹2,430), Premium (₹2,860), and Green Eco (₹2,990) — all fully transparent with no hidden charges.

Do you handle CMDA and DTCP approvals?+

Yes. We provide complete documentation support for CMDA/DTCP approvals. The approval fees are borne by the client, but our team handles all technical drawings, coordination, and submission processes.

What is a Defect Liability Bond and do you provide it?+

A Defect Liability Bond is a legally binding document that commits the contractor to rectify any structural defects within the specified period at no cost to the owner. Morning Star provides a 5-year bond aligned with RERA structural defect standards — issued at handover.

Can Morning Star assist with home loan processing?+

Absolutely. We provide all technical documents required by banks including structural drawings, BOQ, cost estimates, and stage completion certificates needed for home loan disbursement at each construction milestone.

How long does it take to build a standard 2000 sq. ft. house?+

A typical 2000 sq. ft. residential build takes 10–14 months depending on design complexity, number of floors, and approval timelines. We provide a detailed project schedule with milestones before construction begins.
