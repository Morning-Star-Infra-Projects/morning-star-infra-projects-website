REPAIR CAPABILITIES

Our Repair & Retrofitting Arsenal

Micro-Concreting
Precision injection and micro-concrete application to restore degraded concrete sections. Restores full structural capacity of damaged columns, beams, and slabs without demolition.
Carbon FRP Wrapping
Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer wrapping of columns and beams for seismic retrofitting and load capacity enhancement. Dramatically extends structural life without adding dead load.
Structural Jacketing
RCC, steel, or GFRP jacketing of columns and beams to increase cross-section and load capacity. Ideal for buildings undergoing additional floor construction or usage change.
Crack Injection & Repair
Epoxy and polyurethane resin injection for structural and non-structural cracks. Halts moisture ingress, prevents rebar corrosion, and seals the concrete matrix permanently.
Anti-Carbonation Coating
Specialized coatings to halt the carbonation process in aging concrete, protecting rebar from corrosion and extending the building's service life by 20–30 years.
Structural Engineering Design
Forensic structural analysis, load assessment, and retrofitting design by our post-graduate engineers. Includes issuance of Form B Structural Stability Certificate under TN law.
OUR PROCESS

From Diagnosis to Certification

1. Visual Inspection & Distress Survey
Our engineers conduct a comprehensive visual survey, mapping spalling, cracking, efflorescence, and rebar exposure across the structure.
2. NDT Testing & Concrete Core Analysis
UPV, rebound hammer, and core testing are conducted using our owned equipment per IS 13311 and IS 516 standards.
3. Structural Assessment Report
Detailed engineering report outlining concrete strength, rebar condition, and structural adequacy — with photographic evidence.
4. Repair Specification & BOQ
A complete repair methodology, material specifications, and transparent bill of quantities — before a single rupee is committed.
5. Supervised Repair Execution
All repair work executed by trained applicators under the direct supervision of our structural engineers.
6. Form B Certification
Post-repair, we issue the Structural Stability Certificate (Form B) under the Tamil Nadu Public Buildings Act — legally certifying the building's restored integrity.
