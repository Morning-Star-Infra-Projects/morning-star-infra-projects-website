Morning Star Infra Projects

Our Heritage

Morning Star Company was established in 1957 by Mr. Dharmaraj as an asafoetida manufacturing enterprise. Through the strategic leadership of the family's successive generation Mr. D. Thenraj B.com, the company scaled into a dynamic agro-products conglomerate, managing everything from sourcing and processing to the global distribution of agricultural goods.

A New Chapter

Building upon a decades-old foundation of trust, third-generation entrepreneur Mr. Kasi Ram Kumar (M.Tech, MBA) integrated the group's core values with his passion for infrastructure. This led to the inception of Morning Star Infra Projects—a subsidiary specializing in turnkey building services for:

  • Residential Communities

  • Institutional Facilities

  • Commercial Hubs

  • Hospitality Industries
